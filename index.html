Services

Infrastructure, Steel Works & Heavy Equipment Rental

Over the past fifteen years we have been doing many big and small Infrastructure Projects such as : Cut & Fills, Roads, Gradings, Retaining Walls, Fences, Concrete Pipe Drainages, Bridges and Steel Works. We also have our own Excavators, Bulldozers, Backhoe, Vibro, Motor Graders, Loaders, Trailers and Dump Trucks to support our own projects and available to rent upon request

Industrial & Commercial Building Construction and Maintenance

Our Building Department have been doing great jobs at building : Warehouses, Offices, Factories, Houses, Kiosks, Town Houses, and Social & Religius Buildings. Since 2004, Bangun Bumi Sejahtera has been entrusted to do factory and building maintenance for some big multi-national companies

Supply of Raw Material

We are the only distributor that have DO materials of PT Sudamanik in Tangerang for more than thirty years for Split 1-2, Split 2-3, Screening, Stone Ash (Abu Batu), Base Coarse (Makadam), River Stone (Batu Belah) and Lime Stone. We also provide Rangkas White Sand when the stock is available

ABOUT

PT Bangun Bumi Sejahtera was established in Tangerang-Indonesia on August 13, 2004. General contractor for infrastructure, steel & building works, heavy equipment rentals and supplier of raw materials are our main businesses. The company practices a trust-relationship business approach to clients, suppliers, business partners and staff. The company also has a strong commitment to reasonably meeting costumer’s expectations of performance, and a desire to achieve a fair and equitable conclusion to all business transactions. To meet our clients’ needs, our professional skilled engineering staffs are always ready for design consultation, made value engineering analyze for alternative layout and provide construction details. Currently, PT Bangun Bumi Sejahtera has been reputed as a trusted developing company and commited to emerge as a leader in its field.
